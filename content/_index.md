Hello there! My name is Stellae. It means "Of The Stars" in Latin. :]

My main interests are the Nix build tool, low-level programming, and programs that look cool and work well. My goal is to create and program for embedded devices to use in scientific data collection, specifically within marine or extraterrestrial environments.

I also shoot [MiniDV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DV_(video_format)), play guitar, and write a little. I'm a part of the site team for [kglw.net](https://kglw.net), the King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard fan site.

I care deeply about FOSS, using technology to help others, and the democratization of knowledge. I fight for the users!
