Hello there! My name is Stellae. It means "Of The Stars" in Latin. :]

My main interests are low-level programming and the Nix build system. My career goal is to create and program for embedded devices to use in scientific data collection, specifically within marine or extraterrestrial environments.

I also shoot [MiniDV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DV_(video_format)), play guitar, and [write a little](@/blog/_index.md). I'm a part of the volunteer site team for [kglw.net](https://kglw.net), the [King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_Gizzard_%26_the_Lizard_Wizard) fan site.

I care deeply about FOSS, using technology to help others, and the democratization of knowledge. I fight for the users!
